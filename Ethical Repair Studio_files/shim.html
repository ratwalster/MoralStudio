The Mycelial Network

Individual conversations are the seeds. The soil where they grow into a thriving forest is the Mycelial Platform. This is our vision for a Meso-level ecosystem: a multi-tenant platform where communities of shared mindset can form their own independent "dojos." This architecture is structurally anti-fragile, promoting diversity of thought while fostering connection.

Mycelial Platform Architecture

Dojo A (SRE Team)
Shared Ecosystem (R-ADRs, Events)
Dojo B (Climate Group)

Each dojo is autonomous but connected through a shared substrate of tools and values.

Governance: The Restorative ADR

The core governance tool of the platform is the Restorative Architectural Decision Record (R-ADR). It elevates a standard technical document into an instrument of ethical deliberation.

1. Human Context

Requires teams to explicitly map the potential impact of a decision on individuals (micro), group norms (meso), and broader systemic forces (macro).

2. Kintsugi Plan

Requires teams to proactively identify potential harms and outline a plan for mitigation and repair.

3. Future Story

Asks teams to articulate a hopeful vision for the future this decision enables.
